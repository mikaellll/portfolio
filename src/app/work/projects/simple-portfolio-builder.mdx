# 1. Business Context

Deezer is a mid-sized global music streaming company competing with Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon Music.  
In 2025, Deezer faces **high content costs, margin pressure, and strong competition**, requiring enhanced financial monitoring and management control systems.

This case study presents a financial performance analysis and a management control framework supported by Power BI dashboards.

---

# 2. Financial Overview (2025)

## Key Financial Metrics (Illustrative)

- **Total Revenue:** ~$550M  
- **Operating Margin:** ~3.2%  
- **Cash & Cash Equivalents:** ~$120M  
- **Total Debt:** ~$90M  
- **Free Cash Flow:** Slightly positive but volatile  

**Key Insight:** Deezer shows moderate revenue growth but low profitability due to high licensing and marketing costs.

---

# 3. Key Risks Identified (Management Control Perspective)

## Content Cost Pressure  
- Music licensing costs represent a major share of revenue (high variable cost structure).  
- Margins remain structurally low.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Inflation  
- Marketing expenses increasing to compete with major platforms.  
- Risk of negative lifetime value (LTV/CAC imbalance).

## Revenue Concentration  
- Dependence on subscription revenue with limited diversification.  
- Advertising revenue remains underdeveloped.

## Churn and Subscription Volatility  
- High churn rates affect recurring revenue predictability and cash flow stability.

Provide a high-level financial performance overview for CEO and CFO decision-making.
